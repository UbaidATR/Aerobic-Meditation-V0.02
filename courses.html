Don'ts of Meditative Motion:
  • Don't Rush:
    Meditative motion is about slowing down and being present. Avoid rushing through the practice.

    Everyone's meditative motion journey is unique. Avoid comparing your progress to others and focus on your own experience.

Feldenkrais Method

Feldenkrais Method

The Feldenkrais Method is a mind-body approach to movement and self-awareness. Through gentle exercises, it aims to improve flexibility, posture, and overall well-being.

Types of Feldenkrais Method
  1. Awareness Through Movement (ATM)
  2. Functional Integration (FI)

Dos for Feldenkrais Method:
  • Practice Mindful Awareness:

  • Start Slowly:
    Begin with gentle, slow movements to avoid strain or injury.
  • Listen to Your Body:

  • Be Patient:
